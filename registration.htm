Registration process

We encourage you to register early. Not only is it cheaper for you but it makes the lives of the organisers much easier.

Here's how to purchase your ticket and register

  1. Call NoFitState's hotline on 02920 221330 to purchase your tickets. The line is open Monday-Friday from 0900 untill 1630. You will be given a link to a registration form and a payment referrence number.
  2. Open the supplied link to the registration form and fill in your details. You will need your payment reference number that you received earlier
