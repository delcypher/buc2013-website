How to get there

The locations mentioned in this section are marked on the map above.
  • Car:

    Plan your route using your favourite mapping application (e.g. Google Maps) or good old physical maps. Parking will be available at NoFitState (at the back) throughout the weekend and at "Cardiff and Vale College" during Saturday.

  • Train:

    Cardiff Central station is there nearest train station. See National Rail to plan your route.

  • Cycle:

    Use your favourite cycle mapping application (e.g. Cycle Streets) or good old physical maps to plan your route.

How to get there

The locations mentioned in this section are marked on the map above.
  • Car: Plan your route using your route using your favourite mapping application (e.g Google Maps) or good old physical maps.
  • Bus/Walk:

    Travelling to Cardiff and Vale College from NoFitState building

    1. Walk from NoFitState building to the Bus Stop on Bute Terrace
    2. Take either...
      • the 44 bus (Cardiff city centre - St Mellons). Get off at Trowbridge Road (20-30 minute journey)
      • the 45 bus (Cardiff city centre - St Mellons). Get off at GreenwayRoad (near Trowbridge road junction - see map)
    3. Walk from Trowbridge Road/Greenway Road bus stop to Cardiff and Vale College
    For detailed times see the TravelineCymru website

    Travelling from Cardiff and Vale College to NoFitState building

    1. Walk from Cardiff and Vale College to bus stop on Greenway road near the junction between Trowbridge road and Greenway road
    2. Take the 44 or 45 bus to (St Mellons - Cardiff city centre). Get off at Bute Terrace (30-35 minute journey)
    3. Walk from Bute Terrace to NoFitState building
    For detailed times see the TravelineCymru website
