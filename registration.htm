Tickets

The rider ticket covers the cost of all workshops and also gives entrance to all competitions, social events and displays throughout the weekend. The guardian ticket gives access to the social events and displays througout the weekend. Ticket prices will be...

  • £35 for riders until March 21st
  • £45 for riders after March 21st
  • £10 for guardians. This ticket cannot be used to compete in events, for that you must buy a rider ticket.

Please note that you must be 16 years old or over to attend on your own. If you're younger than 16 you must be accompanied by an adult guardian.

Registration process for guardians

The guardian ticket exists to make it cheaper for guardians to attend BUC with their child. This ticket CANNOT be used to compete in events. Guardians do not pay for their ticket in advance but instead pay for their ticket upon entrance to BUC2013. We do ask though that you register online so that we know that you're coming. To register fill in the online registration form (the same one that was used for your child) and put GUARDIAN as your payment reference number.

Registration process for riders

We encourage you to register early. Not only is it cheaper for you but it makes the lives of the organisers much easier.

Here's how to purchase your ticket and register

  1. Call NoFitState's hotline on 02920 221330 to purchase your tickets. Payment can be by debit or credit card. The line is open Monday-Friday from 0930 until 1700. You will be given a link to a registration form and a payment referrence number in an e-mail (you may have to wait to the end of the working day to receive it). We've been hearing from some people that their e-mails are ending up in there spam folder. If you haven't recevied your e-mail by the end of the day please check there first. If it isn't there please contact us by e-mail and we'll sort it out.
  2. Open the supplied link to the registration form and fill in your details. You will need your payment reference number that you received earlier. If you have bought multiple tickets you will need to fill in a form for each person.
  3. At the start of BUC come to NoFitState during the registration period (Friday 24th May 1800-2330) and register so we know that you're here and so you can complete NoFitState's short medical form. There may also be an early Saturday morning registration period for late arrivals but that is not yet confirmed.

What if I decide after registering that I want to change which events I do?

This isn't a problem simply e-mail us and tell us what you want to do instead.
