Tickets

The ticket covers the cost of all workshops and also gives entrance to all competitions, social events and displays throughout the weekend. Ticket prices will be...

  • £35 for riders until March 21st
  • £45 for riders after March 21st
  • £10 for guardians. This ticket cannot be used to compete in events, for that you must buy a rider ticket.

Please note that you must be 16 years old or over to attend on your own. If you're younger than 16 you must be accompanied by an adult guardian.

Registration process for guardians

The guardian ticket exists to make it cheaper for guardians to attend BUC with their child. This ticket CANNOT be used to compete in events. Guardians do not pay for their ticket in advance but instead pay for their ticket upon entrance to BUC2013. We do ask though that you register so that we know that you're coming. To register fill in the registration form (the same one that was used for your child).

Registration process for riders

We encourage you to register early. Not only is it cheaper for you but it makes the lives of the organisers much easier.

Here's how to purchase your ticket and register

  1. Call NoFitState's hotline on 02920 221330 to purchase your tickets. The line is open Monday-Friday from 0900 until 1630. You will be given a link to a registration form and a payment referrence number.
  2. Open the supplied link to the registration form and fill in your details. You will need your payment reference number that you received earlier. If you have bought multiple tickets you will need to fill in a form for each person.
